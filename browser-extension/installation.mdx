## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Your Browser's Web Store">
    - For Chrome, download the [Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/critique-autonomous-searc/dilehbkmjigbpdjlpaglemebmheoilnm) on the Chrome Web Store.
    - For Edge, download the [Edge Extension](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/critique-autonomous-searc/npkalapdljfkockjgholncmogpiphfle) on the Edge Add-ons page.
    - For Firefox, download the [Firefox Extension](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/critique-autonomous-search/) on the Firefox Add-ons page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Browser">
    Click Install or Add to Browser and confirm the installation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pin Extension">
    Click the puzzle piece icon in your browser toolbar, then pin Critique AI for easy access
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during installation:

1. Make sure your browser is up to date
2. Try disabling other extensions that might conflict
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Contact our [support team](mailto:support@critique-labs.ai) for assistance
