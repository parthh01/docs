## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Chrome Web Store">
    Navigate to the [Critique AI Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/critique) on the Chrome Web Store
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Browser">
    Click "Add to Chrome" and confirm the installation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pin Extension">
    Click the puzzle piece icon in your browser toolbar, then pin Critique AI for easy access
  </Step>
</Steps>

## System Requirements

- Chrome 88 or later
- Edge 88 or later
- Other Chromium-based browsers (version 88+)

## Post-Installation Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account">
    Click the Critique AI icon and sign up for a free account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Preferences">
    Choose which features you want to enable:
    - Automatic fact-checking
    - Image analysis
    - Text analysis
    - Tab context
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Browsing">
    Refresh your tabs to activate Critique AI's features
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during installation:

1. Make sure your browser is up to date
2. Try disabling other extensions that might conflict
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Contact our [support team](mailto:support@critique-labs.ai) for assistance
