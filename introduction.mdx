## Quick Setup

Get started with Critique in just three simple steps:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Pin Extension" icon="thumbtack">
    Pin Critique to your browser toolbar for easy access
  </Card>

  <Card title="Log In" icon="right-to-bracket">
    Open the extension popup and sign in to your account
  </Card>

  <Card title="Refresh" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Refresh your open tabs and you're ready to go!
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
