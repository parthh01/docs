# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 27, 2025  
**Last Updated:** July 27, 2025

## Overview

check-ride.ai is operated by Starman LLC ("we," "us," or "our") and powered by Critique AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our aviation training platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and account credentials
- **Training Parameters:** Aircraft type, operating location, endorsements/ratings sought
- **Payment Information:** Billing details and transaction history (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Voice Data:** Audio recordings of your practice sessions with our AI examiner
- **Session Data:** Training session transcripts, responses, and performance metrics
- **Usage Information:** How you interact with our platform, session duration, and feature usage
- **Technical Information:** IP address, browser type, device information, and access logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our AI-powered checkride simulation service
- Generate personalized feedback and performance assessments
- Maintain your training history and progress tracking
- Process payments and manage your account
- Provide customer support
- Improve our AI models through anonymized and aggregated data analysis
- Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

## Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Strict access limitations to authorized personnel only
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Cloud-based infrastructure with robust security protocols
- **Regular Audits:** Ongoing security assessments and updates

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not and will never share your personal information with:**
- Advertisers or marketing companies
- Flight schools or training organizations
- The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) or other regulatory bodies
- Third-party analytics services (beyond anonymized, aggregated data)

**We may share information only in these limited circumstances:**
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations or court orders
- To protect our rights, safety, or property
- In connection with a business transfer (with continued privacy protections)

## AI Model Improvement

We may use your session data to improve our AI models, but only after:
- Removing all personally identifiable information
- Anonymizing voice patterns and speech characteristics
- Aggregating data to prevent individual identification
- Implementing additional privacy safeguards

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Training data retained while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data automatically deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deletion Requests:** We honor all user deletion requests within 30 days
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information and training data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your training history and performance data
- Opt out of AI model improvement programs
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## Children's Privacy

check-ride.ai is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## International Users

If you access check-ride.ai from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

- **Support:** support@check-ride.ai
